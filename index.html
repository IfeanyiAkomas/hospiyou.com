What is Hospiyou?

Hospiyou is an AI powered global telemedical corporation that assists people get medically tested and diagnosed from anywhere and at anytime. Hospiyou serves as a middle man between patients and Medical facilities (Hospitals, Pharmacies, Laboratories, etc), an emergency response ambulance system, and as a system that helps to facilitate medical services.

Our Key Features

Medical check up / diagnosis

With Hospiyou, you will be able to take medical check ups at anywhere and anytime with ease

Emergency ambulance system

With Hospiyou, you will be able to request for an ambulance, in cases of emergency

Adequate health education

Hospiyou will help you develop healthy life plans (food, routines, etc) depending on your medical history

Healthy life plans and routines

Locate hospitals nearby, and with your permission, your medical data will be sent to hospitals

Locate nearby hospitals

Locate hospitals nearby, and with your permission, your medical data will be sent to a hospital even before you arrive.

Talk to specialists easily

With Hospiyou, you will be able to fix appointments with specialists, and get adequate help and attention.
